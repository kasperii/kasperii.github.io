Edge Hamburg, is the annual conference in the fall. I worked as the Art Director for the event 2018.

This included producing a brand book for the yearly concept and the design.

The typeface and the colours are closely related to the brand concept of Netlight.

The pattern was generated and featured different variations with similar shape.

The brand book featured how to utalise the venue screens and a short feature animation that was run between speakers.
