Keywords: Qualitative Study, Concept Design, Workshops, 3D Design

Problem: Smart fridges are monolithic and costly, with technical features that fit narrow user practices.

Solution: A set of DIY technologies to allow ‘smart’ functionallity to any fridge.

These concepts borrow aesthetically from existing practices of self-assembly - such as Ikea. The project included four concept ideas, visualised through 3D renders and isometric assebly guides, collected together as a booklet to be shared with participants for feedback. Future step is to construct one of these designs and to share DIY kits to these participants for evaluation of the concepts.
