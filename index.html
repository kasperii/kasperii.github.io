about myself

This is my humble webpage. Please feel free to check out my work below. I am in my fifth and last year as a PhD Student in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at Stockholm University. During this PhD, I have published several articles in competitive Conferences on topics including self-tracking technologies, sleep technologies, gaze interaction, virtual reality, and kitchen technologies. My PhD has also included a visit at Copenhagen University and Tokyo University. I did my Master's in Computer Science and a Bachelor's in Media Technology, one semester at Aalto University (puhun Suomea!) and one year as a guest student at Keio University (日本語をうまく書くことができませんが、少し読むことができます!)
